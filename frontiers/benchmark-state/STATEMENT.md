# Statement

Each record here is an AI-for-science benchmark claim (MiniF2F, ProteinGym) held at its verification state: dataset version pinned, eval harness available, checkpoint released, leakage audited, independently re-run. The frontier's content is the trust state of each claim, not the score — an unreproduced claim stays flagged as an open verification obligation, and the leakage and track-conflation hazards are recorded alongside the claims they bound.

— William Blair, 2026-06-11

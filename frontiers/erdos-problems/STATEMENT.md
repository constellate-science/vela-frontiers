# Statement

A catalogue of problems posed by or attributed to Paul Erdős, held as a signed frontier record: statements, Lean formalizations, attempts, open obligations, and banked obstructions. Worked problems carry graded results checked by named frozen verifiers (LRAT unsat certificates, Lean kernel proofs, exhaustive recomputation), and proven dead ends are banked with their certificates so no producer re-grinds them. The conjectures themselves remain open unless the record says otherwise.

— William Blair, 2026-06-11
